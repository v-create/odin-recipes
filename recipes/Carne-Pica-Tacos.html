Carne Picada Tacos

Description

This authentic mexican recipe takes you back to the motherland with fresh ingredients and zinging taste. Perfect to get a little messy with family and friends andhave a great time.

Ingredients

  • Eight 7-inch tacos
  • 2 cups prepared carne picada
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tbsp salsa
  • 1/2 lime in wedges

Steps

  1. Heat tortillas directly on a gas grill or in the microwave. Place 2 tablespoons carne picada on each tortilla. Top with onions, cilantro, and salsa. Serve with lime wedge.
